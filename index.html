/04 About

A short introduction, mostly true.

designer, occasional astrologer, full-time chai drinker.

It all started with curiosity,

Growing up around technology, Computer Science felt like the obvious path. But I was always more interested in understanding people and how they interact with the world around them than writing code itself.

Somewhere along the way, I discovered design. What drew me in wasn't the visual side of it, but the idea of making sense of complexity and turning it into something people could easily understand and trust.

Today, I work on products in the world of payments and fintech, designing experiences that sit at the intersection of business, technology, and human behaviour. I enjoy untangling messy systems, finding patterns, and making things feel simpler than they are.

Outside of work, that same curiosity shows up in different ways — exploring new places, experimenting with recipes, building small side projects, or disappearing into rabbit holes about how products and systems work.

I don't always have a plan, but curiosity has been a pretty reliable compass so far.
