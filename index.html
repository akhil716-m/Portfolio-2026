About Me

The story so far.

Hello, I'm Akhil.

I'm a Product Designer at Juspay, where I work on enterprise payment products. My work focuses on simplifying complex workflows and building experiences that help businesses make better decisions.

Over the last few years, I've worked across HRMS and fintech, designing experiences for both frontline workers and enterprise teams. While the domains have changed, the challenge has remained surprisingly similar: making complex systems easier to understand, navigate, and act on.

Looking back, many of the things I enjoy about my work today have been shaped through design — the curiosity to explore how things work, the creativity to imagine possibilities, and the discipline to solve problems with clarity and intent.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me exploring new places, experimenting with recipes, or learning about how products, businesses, and systems evolve. These days, that curiosity has also extended into building a few things.
