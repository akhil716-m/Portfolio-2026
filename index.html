/04 About

A short introduction, mostly true.

designer, occasional astrologer, full-time chai drinker.

I'm a product designer who enjoys making complex things feel simple.

Coming from a Computer Science background, I didn't originally see myself becoming a designer. During the COVID years, while exploring paths beyond what I was studying, I discovered design and found myself drawn to the unique blend of creativity, problem-solving, and human understanding it offered.

What began as curiosity soon became a space to explore new ideas, think differently, and build things with intention. Over time, that curiosity grew into a passion, and eventually a career. Since then, I've been learning, building, and refining my craft as a self-taught designer.

What excites me most about design is the process of understanding. I enjoy breaking things down, questioning assumptions, and getting to the core of what truly matters before exploring solutions. Over time, this approach has shaped a belief I return to often: simplicity is earned through understanding.

Today, I focus on creating experiences that balance simplicity, empathy, and thoughtful decision-making — products that not only work well, but feel intuitive and considerate to the people using them.

Outside of work, I enjoy travelling, playing badminton and cricket, and exploring new places. I've covered much of South India so far and am looking forward to my first international trip.
