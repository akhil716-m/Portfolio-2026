It started with curiosity, not a plan.

02 How I think

I take things apart before I put them back together.

I've always enjoyed making sense of things. Whether it's a product, a workflow, or a messy problem, my instinct is to break it down, question assumptions, and understand what's really happening beneath the surface.

Over time, this became the foundation of how I design. The best solutions rarely come from adding more. They come from understanding more.
